Biography

MD. SAKIBUL HAQUE completed his B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB), Dhaka.

He completed his H.S.C. from B.A.F. Shaheen College, Kurmitola, and passed S.S.C. from Banani Bidyaniketan School and College.

His passion lies in robotics, which led him to form a robotics team called “The Mavericks” with some of his friends in 2019. Together, they participated in various robotics competitions, including "Robo Soccer."
